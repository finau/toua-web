Delete Your Data

Effective date: September 13, 2025

We respect your privacy. If you no longer wish to use Toua and want your personal information removed, you may request deletion of your account and associated data.

1. What happens when you delete your account

  • Your profile information (name, email, photo, status) will be permanently removed.
  • Your chat messages will be deleted from our database, or anonymized where complete deletion is not technically possible.
  • Your access to the App and its features will be revoked.

2. How to request deletion

You can request deletion in either of the following ways:

  • From the App: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account and follow the confirmation steps.
  • By email: Send a request from your registered email address to fkaufusi@gmail.com with the subject line Delete My Account.

3. How long does it take?

We will process your request within 30 days of receiving it. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.

4. Exceptions

In some cases we may retain certain information as required by law (for example, financial transaction records). Such data will be securely stored and deleted once no longer legally required.
